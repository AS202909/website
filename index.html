Autonomous System 202909

Independent Network Research Lab

Exploring Anycast Architectures & Sovereign European Networking

Mission

AS202909 is an independent, non-commercial research initiative dedicated to the study of internet routing protocols and large-scale infrastructure resilience. The project serves as a technical testing ground for BGP configurations, Anycast distribution, and advanced network security models.

In an increasingly centralized internet environment, our lab investigates decentralized infrastructure models that prioritize data sovereignty and routing transparency within the European jurisdiction.

Lab Status

Current Research Areas

Anycast Optimization

Analysis of latency-based routing to geographically distributed nodes using advanced BGP community steering.

Traffic Resilience

Evaluating infrastructure hardening and packet-flow behavior through the implementation of open-source toolchains like XDP and eBPF.

Data Sovereignty

Measuring path diversity to ensure critical data streams remain within defined legal and geographic European boundaries.
